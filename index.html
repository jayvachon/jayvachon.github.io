NewsPix
Matt Carroll, Catherine D'Ignazio, MIT Media Lab

Conceived by Catherine D'Ignazio and Matt Carroll, NewsPix is a Chrome extension that displays a breaking news story every time the user opens a new tab. The goal of the project is to help drive engagement to small and local new organizations at a time when most people get their news from social media feeds.

Our first partner is the Keene Sentinel.

Risk Horizon

A game that teaches the key concepts of the World Bank's "World Development Report 2014, Risk and Opportunity: Managing Risk for Development." It was designed for the final section of an online course, and was played by over 7,000 people in 178 countries in just one week.

As part of the project I parsed the gameplay data so that the game's strength as a learning tool could be studied. Lab director Eric Gordon wrote about the results of that data here.
